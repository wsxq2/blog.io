## Unix diff
1. normal

   <pre>
   $ diff f1 f2
   
   4c4
   < a
   ---
   > b
   </pre>
	
2. context
   <pre>
   $ diff -c f1 f2
   
   *** f1 2012-08-29 16:45:41.000000000 +0800
   --- f2 2012-08-29 16:45:51.000000000 +0800
   ***************
   *** 1,7 ****
    a
    a
    a
   !a
    a
    a
    a
   --- 1,7 ----
    a
    a
    a
   !b
    a
    a
    a
   </pre>
3. unified
   <pre>
   $ diff -u f1 f2
   
   --- f1 2012-08-29 16:45:41.000000000 +0800
   +++ f2 2012-08-29 16:45:51.000000000 +0800
   @@ -1,7 +1,7 @@
    a
    a
    a
   -a
   +b
    a
    a
    a
   </pre>
